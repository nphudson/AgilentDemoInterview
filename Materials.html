Explanation of Process

Instead of trying to create a mock-up of an IMS, this page will be used to explain the process of how this was all created.
First, I drew a rough sketch of the design elements that I wanted the dashboard to have.

Next, I found a rough outline of a dashboard that was open source and fairly basic that I thought I could try to modify.
The link to that example is here.
The developments I made as as follows:
  • Switched the color of the top bar to fit with the theme of the company.
  • Added the company logo in the top left with a link to the main dashboard page.
  • Created a notification bell with a popup.
  • Developed pages that fit with the theme of what I assumed would be areas that Agilent would like to monitor to put on the sidebar.
  • Found custom icons for all of the pages.
  • Made all of the pages link to one another.
  • Produced content for all of the pages.
